Authoring Notes
Schema stability

No content schema changes are required for this system. The design is driven entirely from existing fields: title, description, pubDate, updatedDate, tags, featuredImage, and markdown structure.

Critical content hooks

`featuredImage` powers the article hero figure. The first body image remains a fallback for archive/social previews. `h2` headings power the table of contents. Word count drives read-time labels across archive and article surfaces.
